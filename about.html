    Dr. Fazlullah Pechuho

    M.B.B.S, M.B.A, (AUD)


    Chief Operating Officer

    Dr. Fazlullah Pechuho envisaged to reimage the concepts of Health Services, Medicines and Medical Devices delivery at their best, hence the incorporation of ALTIMI Biosciences (Pvt) Ltd took place in order to achieve the goals.

    Dr. Fazlullah Pechuho brings to ALTIMI more than 30 years of experience in Health Services, Education and Administration disciplines acquired while being at Senior Management levels during his outstanding carrier.
    Before ALTIMI Dr. Fazlullah Pechuho served as Secretary Health, Education, Finance, Communication and Works (Govt. of Sindh), Director Finance (TDAP & Civil Aviation, Govt. of Pakistan) mainly responsible for Strategic Planning, Budgetary Control, Monitoring, and work-force Management besides, Import and Supply Chain management which are essential tools for any business to success.

    Achievements - Health & Education Discipline

    World Health Organization:

    Liaise with WHO for eradication of life-threatening diseases.

    Cardiac Chest Pain Units:

    Establishment of Cardiac Chest Pain Units all over Sindh Province.

    Pediatric Emergency Centers:

    Establishment of children / pediatric emergency centers under Public Private Partnership mode with the help / under the direct supervision of Child Life foundation (CLF). They are all by no means equivalent to the Canadian Children Emergency Centers.

    Satellite Units for Cardiovascular Diseases:

    Establishment of the satellite units of NICVD all over Sindh to cater the services of Angiography including Angioplasty and other services pertaining to cardiovascular diseases free of cost to the people living in Sindh, Pakistan.

    World Bank:

    Managed World Bank sponsored Sindh Education Reform Program (SERP) including Nutrition Support Program, National Immunization Support and Lady Health Worker Program.

    Asian Development Bank:

    With the help of Asian Development Bank, Public Private Partnership (PPP) tool was made robust / strengthened to bring more investment in Sindh under PPP mode particularly in Education sector.

    USAID:

    Supervised and managed to establish 106 Schools under the direct guidelines of USAID, being the biggest investment done by USAID in Sindh and some of these schools were made functional under Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode as well.

    JICA:

    Projects Liaison for establishment of 60 state of the art Schools in Sindh.

    Syed Tanveer Hussain Musavi

    B.E (Civil)


    Director

    Syed Tanveer Hussain Musavi holds Degree in Civil Engineering discipline and started his carrier as Assistant Engineer with Communication & Works Department, Government of Sindh in the year 1985.
    He remained Supervising / Controlling Officer from 2008 to 2016 and from 2016 to 2019 resumed his responsibilities as Chief Engineer later became Director General Design to implement and monitor the developmental projects in line with set-forth standard rules, regulation and procedures.
    He has also discharged the prime responsibilities being Project Director to execute and supervise the construction of (a) 300 bed Mother & Childcare Hospital (b) National Institute of Cardio Vascular Disease (NICVD) in Province of Sindh (Pakistan).
    His executive managerial experience is diffused over the period of more than 3 (three) decades.
